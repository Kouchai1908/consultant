The Lobbyist is a policy-level consultancy founded by two Timorese professionals driven by a shared commitment to sustainable change. We specialize in consultancy, scientific analysis, and technical expertise across trade, investment, economy and law.

Who we are

Built on policy. Grounded in evidence.

We help governments, parliaments, courts, development partners, private entities and civil-society organizations translate ambition into action — through specialized assistance in governance, development, and analysis of legislative and political initiatives.

Our work draws on a decade of direct experience inside Timor-Leste's WTO and ASEAN accession processes, ministerial advisory, and multilateral trade negotiation — and a steady habit of pairing that experience with rigorous economic and legal analysis.

  • International Impact: navigate global trade negotiations with precision and unlock opportunities through international trade and investment agreements.

  • Intellectual Insights: dive into in-depth economic studies and cutting-edge research. Gain legal clarity with expert advice and translation services.
