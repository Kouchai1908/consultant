C-01
Co-founder · Legal

Jorginha Martins

Legal Consultant

Jorginha is a co-founder and consultant at The Lobbyist and currently serves as Legal Advisor to the Coordinating Minister for Economic Affairs in Timor-Leste. She has over a decade of experience across key government institutions — including the Office of the Prime Minister, the Ministry of Health, and the Office of the Coordinating Minister for Economic Affairs — focusing on legal and institutional reforms in public administration, health governance, economic reforms, and trade and investment policy.

Jorginha was part of the WTO trade negotiating team, leading the legislative reform priorities for Timor-Leste's accession to the WTO and ASEAN accession, and continues to contribute to the country's regional economic integration. She completed her lawyer course at the Timor-Leste Legal and Judiciary Training Center, with her internship at the national law firm Da Silva Teixeira & Associados.

In addition to economic policy, She has supported justice-sector and human-rights initiatives — including capacity-building for judges, prosecutors and public defenders on human trafficking, gender-based violence, and child protection through her work with The Asia Foundation. She has collaborated with CEPAD on access to information, lectured in Fiscal Law and Commercial Law at UNTL, and previously served on the board of SABEH, supporting healthcare access in rural areas.

C-02
Co-founder · Economic Policy

Maria Martins da Silva

Economic Policy, Trade, Investment & Environmental Consultant

Maria is a co-founder and consultant at The Lobbyist. She currently serves as Senior Economic Adviser to the Coordinating Ministry for Economic Affairs in Timor-Leste through the Australian Aid programme PROSIVU.

She has more than 10 years of experience in multilateral trade negotiations and economic policy, leading Timor-Leste's trade negotiating team through the WTO accession process to membership, and most recently supporting ASEAN accession and post-accession. Maria is also an international consultant as a TradeWorthy Associate, providing support across the Asia-Pacific — including ASEAN and APEC work on economic, trade, digital-trade and investment matters.

In that capacity she contributed research and technical support to Timor-Leste's E-Commerce Strategic Action Plan, and project management and research for the APEC Digital Provision Handbook. She supported Laos' ASEAN Chairmanship in 2024 by preparing Ministers' remarks for AEC meetings. As Senior Economic Adviser to the Vice Prime Minister of Timor-Leste, Maria has played a key role in shaping economic policy and trade integration strategies, including microeconomic and macroeconomic analysis to inform government decision-making.

Maria has shared her expertise at public lectures and seminars — including at the ISEAS-Yusof Ishak Institute — on the challenges Timor-Leste faces in its journey towards global and regional integration. She previously interned at the World Trade Organization in 2017–2018.
