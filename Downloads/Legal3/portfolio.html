Consultancy service to Heineken Timor-Leste (2025)

Advocacy on Timor-Leste's Fiscal Policy — Tax Reform and Implementation

The Lobbyist partnered with a local consultant to provide services in bridging Heineken Timor-Leste S.A. (HTSA)'s interest to obtain a productive dialogue with key government entities and other related stakeholders to advocate for the tax reform process.

The Lobbyist carried out a communication strategy, research of best practices in the region, data analysis, and other relevant analysis that streamlined the lobbying process with the government through influencing key Government institutions and the National Parliament.

The Lobbyist successfully completed the project by fulfilling HTSA's primary objective.
